Install on iPhone/iPad

iOS requires these steps manually — Apple doesn't allow apps to trigger this automatically:

  1. Make sure you're in Safari (this won't work in Chrome on iOS)
  2. Tap the Share icon ⬆️ in the bottom toolbar
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" in the top right
